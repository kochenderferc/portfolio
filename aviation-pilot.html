Prepare2Go · Piper PA-28 · 2024

As a ferry pilot for Prepare2Go, I flew a Piper PA-28 across the Atlantic Ocean from Florida to Belgium. I'm a certified student and remote pilot with 100+ hours, and I passed the FAA Private Pilot Written exam.

Beyond flying, I'm active in pilot mental health advocacy and in spreading awareness of current aviation legislation and research. I also helped build an RV-12 aircraft with EAA Chapter 20.
